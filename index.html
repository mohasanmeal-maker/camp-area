About FIVDB & Project

Friends in Village Development Bangladesh (FIVDB) is a national development organization established in 1979. The organization works to empower disadvantaged communities through education, health, livelihood, financial services, and environmental protection.

FIVDB is implementing the “Education for Rohingya Refugee Children and Host Community Children in Bangladesh” project under the Education Cannot Wait (ECW) Multi-Year Resilience Programme (MYRP II), with technical support from Plan International Bangladesh (PIB).

The project is currently implemented in Camps 01 East, 03, and 09, with a total of 60 learning facilities. This tool helps identify nearest facilities, distance, and travel time to support efficient field visits and monitoring.
